I am an Associate Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Technology at Peking University. I work on computer systems, with a focus on hardware-software co-design, programmable networks and machine learning systems. My research has received USENIX NSDI Best Paper Award (2018) and USENIX FAST Best Paper Award (2019).
